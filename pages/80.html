Renewable Energy Sources - ECE 80J

My class on Renewable Energy Sources was a very surface level exploration into the viability of current and future renewable energy source options. This class was largely about exposing us to as many different energy generating solutions to the growing problem of demand as possible. As such, many topics were not covered with granular detail and technical information was simplified or omitted. I have chosen to include my Final Report because it was the best work I produced for this class. It was written by me in conjunction with the team I was a part of where we explored the efficacy and viability of various battery technologies.
