Senior Design Project: Tactile Stimulation - ECE 129

I was one member of a two person team in my senior design project class. Our project aimed to simulate virtual reality textures using transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation. Creating the electrical nerve interface required circuit design and modeling, iterative prototyping, additive manufacturing, and microcontroller programming to implement specific waveforms.
